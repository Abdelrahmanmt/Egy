Amenemhat III: Pharaoh of the Middle Kingdom

Amenemhat III

Amenemhat III was one of the most significant pharaohs of Egypt’s Middle Kingdom. Known for his extensive building projects and innovations in mining and canal systems, Amenemhat III’s reign was a time of prosperity and peace.

During his rule, he commissioned the building of the Black Pyramid at Dahshur and is also associated with the construction of the Labyrinth at Hawara. His contributions left a lasting legacy in ancient Egyptian history.
