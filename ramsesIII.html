Ramses III: Pharaoh of the New Kingdom

Ramses III

Ramses III is often regarded as the last great pharaoh of Egypt's New Kingdom. His reign was marked by extensive building projects, military expeditions, and cultural achievements.

He is most famous for his defense against the Sea Peoples and for the construction of the magnificent Temple Medinet Habu, which served as both a mortuary temple and a site for royal worship.
